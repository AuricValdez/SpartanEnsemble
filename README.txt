Spartan Ensemble
____________________________________

Introduction >>>

	Welcome to Spartan Ensemble, the innovative solution designed to revolutionize classroom attendance management at Batangas State University. This system addresses the challenges faced by students and instructors in tracking attendance, offering a streamlined and efficient process through cutting-edge technology. Spartan Ensemble simplifies attendance tracking through an intuitive online portal , reducing administrative burdens and enhancing the accuracy of records. Developed using Agile methodology, this system prioritizes collaboration, adaptability, and continuous improvement to meet the dynamic needs of the university community
____________________________________

Getting Started >>>

1. Install XAMPP on your device.

2. Open XAMPP Control panel and start "Apache" and "MySQL."

3. Copy and Paste the SpartanEnsemble folder in "C:\xampp\htdocs."

4. Open a browser and search "localhost/phpmyadmin."

5. Click on new at the left side of the navigation bar.

6. Name the database as "db_nt3102" and click create.

7. Click Import and browse the file located in this folder, "db_nt3102.sql."

8. Once import is successful, you are finished.

____________________________________

Usage >>>

1. Register a faculty account by opening a browser and typing "localhost/SpartanEnsemble/addFaculty.php" (Note: Ignore this step if the user has already registered a faculty account beforehand into the database).

2. Access the main system by opening a browser and typing "localhost/SpartanEnsemble/login.php".

3. Login to the system with your faculty account credentials.

4. Click "Initiate Room Attendance" to create a room attendance.

5. Fill out the necessary fields (Note: Use a 24-hour time for "Time In" and "Time Out" fields). Once filled, click "Initiate."

6. Have the students scan their QR codes to the camera. Once the system has scanned the qr code. The QR code textfield should be filled with random letters and numbers. Click attend.

7. If students do not have a QR code registered to the database. Click "Register QR Code." Fill in the fields and have the system scan their QR code. Click "Register." Once registered, click "Back."

8. If all present students have scanned their QR codes and have attended to the system. Click "End Attendance."

9. To view the attendees of the class. Click "View Attendance Logs." Select the necessary fields to view the students who attended a specific class which you have initiated in the system. Once fields are filled, clicl "Search."

10. To remove a student from the attendance, click "Unattend" at the right side of the table aligning said student whom you wish to unattend.

11. To navigate back to home page. Click the "Home" icon at the top right of the menu bar. 

12. To navigate back to the Login page. Click the "Logout" icon at the top right of the menu bar (Note: The user should be at home page).
